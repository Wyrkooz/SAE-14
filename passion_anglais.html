My passions

Information technology

I've always had a passion for computers. I've always played video games and wanted to learn more about the technologies that surround me. At the age of 13, I was lucky enough to be able to build my own PC all by myself, to understand a little more about how each component works. What's more, in the third year of secondary school, I did my work placement in a company's IT department. I was able to find out how the IT system works, see the server room and learn a few basics about data protection (RGPD), which later made me want to study in these areas.

Automotive

I've always had a passion for cars. I've always found the mechanics behind each car fascinating. What I also like is that behind each model, behind each brand, there's a whole story that I love to discover. My favourite brands are Mercedes and Porsche and I was lucky enough to go to Stuttgart in Germany to visit the Mercedes Benz Museum and the Porsche Museum. I was able to find out more about the history of each brand, from its creation to the present day.

Football

Like many of us, football has always been much more than just a sport. I've always played football as a hobby, but 1 year ago I joined a club to turn my passion into a real thing. For me, football isn't just a sport, it's an integral part of who I am. This sport teaches us not just to 'run after a ball' but to develop real team cohesion, a sense of mutual support and values and principles that are now part of my personality.
